#3. Tudor Back Bay Automatic Black Dial Men's GMT Pepsi Bezel Watch

The best budget version of the Rolex GMT Master II “Pepsi”, there can be no better candidate than the Tudor Black Bay GMT Pepsi. Why? Because it is the budget version of its bigger, badder, and more revered watch. The Tudor and Rolex companies are inextricably linked, and the brand’s histories run side-by-side. Rolex founder Hans Wilsdorf’s goal with Tudor was to provide a working man’s Rolex at an accessible price. While that business model has experienced peaks and troughs in popularity, it still resonates today. The great thing about Tudor is that you’re probably getting more for your money than you would with a Rolex. With Rolex, you cannot ignore the high premium attached to the name. And while the Rolex product is superior to the Tudor output, the difference is not as vast as the pricing structure would suggest. It means the Tudor Black Bay GMT Pepsi is worth every bit of its $3,950 price tag on Jomashop.com (that’s 5% below RRP). This 41mm wide reference M79830RB-0001 is, without a doubt, a modern classic and, to be honest, more likely to be the watch the real Thomas Magnum would choose if he were starting his private investigator practice today. He wasn’t a luxury-minded guy. His taste was so good that it influenced the rest of the world to follow him.
