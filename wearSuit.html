Dress Watches

Considered the most traditional option for formal wear, a dress watch is a perfect match for a suit. These timepieces are typically slim and subtle, designed to slip easily under the sleeve of a dress shirt. They usually feature a simple face, minimal complications, and are often crafted with a leather strap.
